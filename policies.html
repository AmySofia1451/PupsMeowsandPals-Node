Policies

Meet and Greets

A prior meet and greet must be completed before any stay to ensure everything is a good fit for all parties involved. Meet and greets are usually a total of 30 minutes but can go longer. Additional Meet and Greets can be requested for $15 each. Meet and Greets outside of our service areas may be an additional charge as well.

Pet Emergencies/Vet Information

We require all pet vet information prior to any stay. While emergencies are extremely rare, in the event of a pet emergency, we will contact you immediately and your pet will be taken to your specified veterinarian hospital. In the case that your veterinarian office is closed, we will take your pet to the nearest emergency vet hospital.

Cancellations

Life happens! If notice of prior cancellation is given 48 hours before the stay, payment will not be due. If notice to cancel is given with less than 48 hours notice, a $40 per day will be charged for overnight stays and dog sitting. For dog walks and drop ins, there is no cancellation fee with 24 hours notice. Cancellations within 24 hours will be charged a $5 cancellation fee for each 30 minutes of service booked.

Holidays

For all major holidays, an additional $10 per visit will be added to check in visits, dog walking, and cat sitting. An additional $20/night will be added to all house sitting overnight stays. Holidays include: Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, Christmas, New Years Eve, New Years Day, Memorial Day, Easter, Independence Day, and Labor Day.

Payment

Payment is due in full upon completion of the stay. Pet sitting and overnight stays are charged based on a 24 hour period (e.g. 9 AM - 9 AM). If you need pet care over a 24 hour period, there may be an additional cost. Invoices will be sent via text or email. Payments can be made by the way of check, cash, venmo, or PayPal.

Aggressive Dogs

Dogs will need to be non-aggressive towards other people. Pets with aggressive behavior unfortunately will not be accepted.

Dog Walking In Severe Weather

In the case of very cold, snowy, or extremely hot weather, your pup's walk may be cut short or changed to a check in visit, for their safety.
