Pet Care Services

​

Dog Walking and Potty Breaks

Ever feel rushed running home from work to walk or let out your pup? This is the best service for when you have a very busy work schedule! It's a wonderful way to give your pup exercise, plus some good sniffs! We can make a weekly schedule to provide what is best for you and your furry friends needs!

Dog Sitting

Drop in visits are perfect for pet owners going out of town that don't need someone to stay the night with his or her pets. We can arrange as many visits as you would like to meet your pets' needs. Drop-ins can include walks, watering plants, taking in mail/packages, closing and opening blinds, turning on and off lights. Playtime, dog walking, and cuddles included!

​

Kitty Care (and other Critters!)

A wonderful service for pet parents with cats who are going out of town! Feedings, playtime, litter box scooping, medication needs- are all included! We can provide as many visits for your kitty a day you need while you are away. You can have peace of mind knowing we are here to look after your feline friend!

​
​

Overnight Stays

These are perfect for pet owners going out of town or traveling for work. Let us take care of your pets in the comfort of their own home! House sitting includes potty breaks, daily exercise, playtime, litter box maintenance, tons of love and cuddles, and the security of someone being there overnight to comfort your fur babies. Perfect for pets who need a little more love and attention while you're gone! We am more than happy to give medication or provide the care for pets with special needs. We can accomodate additional requests such as watering plants, opening and closing blinds, bringing in the mail, taking out garbage and recycling, and turning the lights on/off, all for no additional cost!

​

Other In-Home Services

Ask us about other services we can provide! Especially on those long trips away, we are happy to help out around the house! We are happy to provide additional services such as bathing your dogs, bringing in the mail, watering the plants, vaccumming the floors, and tending the garden. Please feel free to mention these tasks during your consultation! We may be able to accomodate these requests for a small additional fee or even at no charge!
